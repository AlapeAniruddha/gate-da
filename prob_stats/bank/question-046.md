- [ ] $7!$
  
- [ ] $4! \times 3!$
  
- [ ] $4! \times 4!$

- [ ] $4! \times 5!$

  

::: {.callout-note title="Hint" collapse=true}
$n$ distinct things can be arranged in $n!$ different ways.




:::

- [ ] $7!$
  
- [x] $4! \times 3!$
